This album is currently being composed and remixed but will be released soon.

The KITCTF artists invite you to Compose Thrilling Flags at this year’s GPN from 31 May, 10:00 UTC to 1 June, 22:00 UTC. If you are unable to come to the concert hall, you can of course grab your keyboard and compose online.

From solo artists through aspirant, cacophonous recorder quartets to orchestras of unlimited size, from Rock stars reiterating their biggest hits to garage DJ’s trying to figure out how autotune works - everyone is welcome to participate. We will hand out masterpieces from a plethora of genres, namely:

  • pieces from the second Viennese school (crypto),
  • hard style/(bare) metal (pwn),
  • decomposing (rev),
  • pop (misc),
  • and arbitrary things remixed on YouTube (web).
